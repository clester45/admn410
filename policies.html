    Compay Policies

    Financing

    We offer multiple financing options for our customers

    Credit- We accept all major credit cards with no price minimum

    Layaway- We offer logn term financing (6 months, 12 months, 18 months) with low rates of interest. *no credit needed*

    We pride ourselves on being very agreeable and friendly with our prices and we will not be undersold. Bring in competitior coupons and lower offers and we will match them.

    Delivery

    We offer delivery to all customers within 30 miles of one of our locations. Cost of delivery is determined upon distance and number of items ordered. Deliveries can be ordered for one time use only or interval uses, order online or call any location.

    Returns/Exchanges

    Returns will be honored for all customers returning their items within 30 days of purchase with a receipt. We will not honor returns without a reciept!
    Exchanges can be made without a receipt, products must be unused and completely sealed to be exchanged.
